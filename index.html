The Founder

Shlok Parikh

19 · Solo Founder · Bhavnagar, India
🏆 Teen Entrepreneur

Hi, I'm Shlok 👋

I'm a 19-year-old solo founder who got tired of buying ebooks and courses that never matched what they promised. I watched my friends waste money on content that had no real knowledge behind it.

So I decided to build the solution myself. PassionStack uses AI to verify every piece of content before it reaches students — so you always know exactly what you're getting.

I recently pitched this at a teen entrepreneur event and got real, raw feedback from fellow students. That gave me the confidence to launch this beta. I'm building this for people like me.

This is my first product. I built everything myself — the Android app, the AI evaluation system, the backend. No team. Just passion and a problem worth solving.
